AT DWC


We believe that “a journey of a thousand miles begin with a single humble step.”

In 2009 Dev Wizards College (DWC) founded and since then it has become our mission to establish academic excellence. To set up with state-of-the-art educational infrastructure in the semi-urban abode of Mankundu – Chandannagar was our goal. In a span of ten years, DWC , has turned into an ideal ecosystem for the growth of academic excellence, in the state of West Bengal.

There is a strong focus of our Management Team to develop faculty resources and to create an environment which is conducive for future corporate leaders. Teaching learning process is the heart of our institute. We believe in learning by doing and in empowering our students so that they are prepared to accept the diverse challenges of the future. We work with the leaders in education growth. We employ the latest pedagogical advancements and utilize modern tools and technologies to deliver our programs.

We firmly believe that our students are going to contribute significantly in nation building and world progress. A right equilibrium in Industry-Academia partnership leads to the final upthrust of a qualified student for his/her quantum leap as Industry Ready Professional. We have a strategic tie-up for bridging the gap and provide best training on the latest technologies within our system. Our Placement networking has created opportunities for our students and we have a strong impetus to take it to new levels of success.
